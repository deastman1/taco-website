Tacos do not take alot of ingredients to prepare. Here are the following ingredients for 2 beef tacos:

  • 1/4 lb Ground Beef

  • 1/4 Onion

  • 1/8 Pepper

  • 1 Clove Garlic

  • 25ml Tomato Sauce

  • 3ml Chilli Powder

  • 2ml Cumin

  • Salt and Pepper

  • Cheese of Choice

  • Tomato

  • Lettuce

  • Salsa
