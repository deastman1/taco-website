Here are the steps to making a taco.

  • Add ground beef into pan over medium heat

    • Cook meat thoroughly to avoid Foodborne Illnesses

  • Dice and add onion, pepper, garlic

    • Add these soon before cooking meat to perfection.

  • Add spices and tomato sauce after meat is fully browned

    • Make sure meat is not red because it will mix with the sauce.

  • Grate cheese

    • Add cheese right after meat is done to melt it.

  • Dice tomato

    • These depend on prefence.

  • Shred lettuce

    • These also depend on preference.

  • Assemble the taco with salsa added

    • Salsa is an important ingredient for flavour and to not dry the mouth.
