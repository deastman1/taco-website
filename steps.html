Here are the steps to making a taco.

  • Add ground beef into pan over medium heat

  • Dice and add onion, pepper, garlic

  • Add spices and tomato sauce after meat is fully browned

  • Grate cheese

  • Dice tomato

  • Shred lettuce

  • Assemble the taco with salsa added
